# Privacy Notice

**Pub Night Picker**
Last updated: April 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

Pub Night Picker is operated by Christopher Hopkins.

If you have questions about this notice or want to exercise your privacy rights, contact:

- Email: [ampubnight@cbeh.uk]

## 2. What This App Does

Pub Night Picker helps users:

- create an account and sign in
- vote in venue polls
- record attendance for events
- receive optional email notifications
- participate in chat where enabled
- manage venues and users where they hold elevated permissions

## 3. Personal Data We Collect

We process the following categories of personal data.

### Account and profile data

- user ID (`uid`)
- name or preferred name
- login email address
- authentication provider
- profile photo URL or custom avatar URL
- notification email address, where provided
- notification preferences

### Participation data

- votes for venues
- attendance responses
- chat messages where chat is enabled
- timestamps and event-linked participation history where required for app operation

### Access and permissions data
- role assignments linked to a user ID
- permission status for administrative or moderation functions

### Operational and support data
- notification request and acknowledgement records
- security, access, and diagnostic logs produced by Firebase, Google Cloud, or hosting infrastructure

## 4. Why We Use Personal Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

- to create and manage user accounts
- to authenticate users and secure the service
- to let users vote and record attendance for events
- to show event participation information to relevant users
- to deliver optional email notifications where a user has enabled them
- to provide chat functionality where enabled
- to administer venues, polls, permissions, and users
- to maintain service integrity, investigate misuse, and protect the app
- to comply with legal obligations where applicable

## 5. Lawful Bases

- **Account creation, sign-in, and core service operation:** legitimate interests in providing the service, or performance of an agreement to use it
- **Venue polling, attendance planning, and event coordination:** legitimate interests
- **Optional direct email notifications:** consent — you can withdraw this at any time via your preferences
- **Security monitoring, abuse prevention, and access control:** legitimate interests
- **Legal obligations:** where applicable

## 6. Who We Share Data With

We may share personal data with service providers that process data on our behalf, including:

- Google Firebase Authentication
- Google Firestore
- Google Cloud Hosting or related infrastructure used to run the app
- email delivery providers used for notification emails, if any

These providers act as processors where they process personal data on our behalf.

We do not sell personal data.

## 7. International Transfers

This app is hosted and operated using Google Firebase and Google Cloud infrastructure. Google may process data in countries outside the UK or EEA. Google's services operate under standard data protection terms and transfer safeguards provided by Google as a data processor.

Any email delivery service used for notifications may also process data outside the UK or EEA in line with that provider's own data protection terms.

## 8. How Long We Keep Data

| Data | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account profile data | Until account deletion, then removed within 30 days |
| Role assignments | Until removed or until account deletion |
| Venue votes and attendance records | Up to 12 months after the event date |
| Chat messages | Up to 12 months after posting |
| Notification request and acknowledgement records | Up to 30 days |
| Application activity logs | Not retained in the database; any logs exist only locally in the browser session |
| Backups | Minimum period required for recovery |


## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

- access your personal data
- correct inaccurate personal data
- erase your personal data
- restrict processing
- object to processing based on legitimate interests
- data portability
- withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
- complain to the relevant supervisory authority

For UK users, this is usually the Information Commissioner's Office.

## 10. How To Exercise Your Rights

You can contact us using the details above to request:

- a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- correction of your profile details
- deletion of your account and associated data
- withdrawal of optional email notification consent

We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

## 11. Automated Decision-Making

This app does not make legally significant decisions based solely on automated processing.

## 12. Security

We use technical and organizational measures intended to protect personal data, including authenticated access controls and role-based restrictions.

No internet service can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 13. Children

This service is intended for adults aged 18 and over. It involves choosing venues including licensed premises, and is not directed at children.

## 14. Changes To This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The latest version will be made available through the app or website.
